# Top Vegan Snack Brands to Try in the UK for 2025 (UK Guide 2025)

**Date:** 2025-12-10  
**Tags:** ["Reviews", "Home", "Vegan"]

## Introduction

As plant-based lifestyles continue to flourish, the array of innovative vegan snacks available across the UK keeps expanding. With interest in ethical eating and sustainable choices on the rise, 2025 has ushered in a remarkable range of snacks that are not only delicious but are also cruelty-free and environmentally conscious. This review delves into the top 10 vegan snack brands that are making waves across the British Isles, providing you with the perfect guide to satisfy your cravings while adhering to a compassionate lifestyle.

## Detailed Mini-Reviews

### 1. Vivera Reimagined Snack Bites

Vivera has long been a pioneer in plant-based options, and their Reimagined Snack Bites are no exception. With a blend of unique spices and high-quality ingredients, these bites offer a delightful crunch and a complex flavour profile. Ideal for on-the-go snacking or as a savoury addition to your lunchbox, they serve as a testament to Vivera's commitment to innovation and taste.

### 2. Higgidy Veggie Sausage Rolls

These indulgent veggie sausage rolls from Higgidy provide a satisfying experience for both vegans and non-vegans alike. The flaky, golden pastry envelops a rich, flavourful filling that combines vegetables and carefully selected herbs. Perfect for picnics or an afternoon treat, their hearty taste makes them a comforting choice year-round.

### 3. M&S Plant Kitchen Sweet Potato Falafels

M&S continues to elevate plant-based snacking with their delectable Sweet Potato Falafels. These falafels are expertly crafted with a hint of spice and the smooth texture of sweet potato, offering both a nutritious and flavoursome treat. Pair them with a salad or dip them in hummus for a versatile bite.

### 4. Whitworths Protein Almond Bakes

Embarking on a healthy snacking journey has never been easier with Whitworths Protein Almond Bakes. High in protein and complemented by the natural sweetness of almonds, these bakes serve as a perfect post-workout snack or an energising boost during a busy day. The balance of nutrition and taste is sure to win you over.

### 5. Graze Smoky BBQ Crunch

Graze combines convenience with irresistible taste in their Smoky BBQ Crunch. This snack offers a cheerful medley of smoky and tangy flavours, coupled with the satisfying crunch of nuts and seeds. Ideal for anyone looking for a bold snack that doesn’t compromise on taste and texture.

### 6. LoveRaw Filled Wafer Bars

These Filled Wafer Bars from LoveRaw are set to delight those with a sweet tooth. Crafted with love and without compromising on quality, each bite reveals a luscious filling encased in crunchy wafer goodness. A guilt-free treat that fits perfectly into a vegan lifestyle, satisfying your chocolate cravings with ease.

### 7. Squirrel Sisters Raspberry Ripple Bites

Squirrel Sisters have created a vibrant blend of indulgence and nutrition with their Raspberry Ripple Bites. These bites stand out not just for their fruity flavour profile but also for their commitment to all-natural ingredients. The bites are both gluten-free and vegan, making them an inclusive snack for all to enjoy.

### 8. Deliciously Ella Cashew & Almond Energy Balls

Synonymous with wholesome ingredients and nourishing recipes, Deliciously Ella's Cashew & Almond Energy Balls provide a convenient snack loaded with energy-boosting properties. The combination of nuts offers a marvellously chewy texture and delicious taste, perfect for powering through your day or a quick pick-me-up.

### 9. Quorn Vegan Salt & Vinegar Fishless Fillets

A testament to Quorn’s expertise in meat-free innovation, their Vegan Salt & Vinegar Fishless Fillets deliver a classic British flavour with a vegan twist. Experience the taste of the seaside with these fillets, offering a crispy exterior and a satisfying, flaky inside. Enjoy them alongside chips to recreate the traditional experience minus the fish.

### 10. Nush Almond Milk Yoghurts

Rounding off the list are the Almond Milk Yoghurts from Nush, ideal for breakfast or a creamy dessert treat. These yoghurts are made with high-quality almond milk, providing a smooth, silky texture alongside a delectable range of flavours. They serve as a perfect dairy-free alternative, nourishing both body and soul.

## Buying Guide

When seeking the perfect vegan snack in 2025, consider the following pointers:

- **Check Ingredients:** Always review the ingredients list to ensure there are no hidden animal-derived ingredients.
  
- **Nutritional Content:** Opt for snacks with well-balanced nutritional profiles, particularly if you rely on them for energy or dietary supplement.

- **Texture and Flavour:** Choose snacks that appeal to your taste preferences, whether you favour sweet, savoury, crunchy, or smooth textures.

- **Ethical Sourcing:** Look for brands that prioritize ethical sourcing and packaging materials to further contribute to sustainable consumption.

## Conclusion

The UK’s vegan snack market in 2025 is brimming with exceptional options suitable for every palate and preference. With taste and ethical considerations at the forefront, this guide serves as both an introduction and an inspiration to explore the diverse world of plant-based snacking. Indulge healthily and ethically with these premier choices, and relish in the satisfaction that your snacking supports a kinder planet.
