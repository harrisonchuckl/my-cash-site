## Buying Guide

When choosing a treadmill in 2025, consider the following key aspects:

- **Motor Power**: Higher CHP (Continuous Horse Power) ratings typically translate to better performance and durability, essential for regular runners.
- **Features**: Look for features such as incline settings, interactive displays, and connectivity options like Bluetooth for a more engaging workout experience.
- **Space**: Ensure the treadmill fits your available space, with foldable options providing versatility for smaller areas.
- **Budget**: Consider your budget and balance it with the features you need, ensuring you invest in quality that meets your fitness goals.
